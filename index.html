The Psyche Rover design team has been tasked with conceiving a robotic explorer to assist in the examination and analysis of the 16 Psyche asteroid. It is hypothesized that the asteroid is mainly metallic in composition, being comprised of metallic iron and nickel. Due to this composition, the asteroid is of significant interest, as scientists believe that Psyche may be the exposed core of an early planet.
